MINIMAL CLEAN SLIDE TEMPLATE
=============================

Design Style: Minimalist, Clean, Simple

DESIGN REQUIREMENTS:
✓ Ultra-clean, minimalist design
✓ Limited color palette: Black (#000000), White (#FFFFFF), Light Gray (#F5F5F5), Single accent color (Blue or Green)
✓ Lots of white space
✓ Minimal text - only essential information
✓ Simple, sans-serif typography
✓ Grid-based layout with strict alignment
✓ 16:9 widescreen format
✓ Modern, sophisticated appearance

CONTENT STRUCTURE:
- Minimal title (centered or left-aligned)
- 1-3 key points maximum
- Use white space generously
- Simple line elements or minimal icons
- Very clean footer

STYLE GUIDELINES:
✓ Maximum white space
✓ Minimal text
✓ Simple geometric shapes only
✓ Black and white primarily
✓ One accent color maximum
✗ NO gradients
✗ NO shadows
✗ NO unnecessary decorations
✗ NO bright colors

COLOR PALETTE:
Primary: Black (#000000)
Secondary: Light Gray (#F5F5F5)
Accent: Single color - Blue (#0066CC) or Green (#00AA44)
Background: White (#FFFFFF)
Text: Black (#000000)

Think: What would Apple, Google, or high-end design studios create?
Less is more. Focus on content, not decoration.
