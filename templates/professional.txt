PROFESSIONAL CONSULTING SLIDE TEMPLATE
======================================

Design Style: BCG/McKinsey Professional

DESIGN REQUIREMENTS:
✓ Subtle, sophisticated design - NO bright colors
✓ Professional color palette: Navy blue (#1F4E78), Charcoal gray (#4A4A4A), White (#FFFFFF), Light gray (#E8E8E8), Teal accent (#2E8B9E)
✓ Minimal, clean layout - maximum white space
✓ Professional typography - serif fonts for titles, sans-serif for body
✓ Grid-based layout
✓ 16:9 widescreen format
✓ Executive-ready appearance

CONTENT STRUCTURE:
- Bold title in navy blue
- 3-5 key points or visual elements
- Minimal text, maximum clarity
- Use simple icons or small diagrams if needed
- Clean footer with slide number

STYLE GUIDELINES:
✗ NO gradients, shadows, or 3D effects
✗ NO bright colors (red, lime green, orange, pink)
✗ NO animations
✓ Flat design only
✓ Generous margins and white space
✓ Professional and sophisticated look

COLOR PALETTE:
Primary: Navy Blue (#1F4E78)
Secondary: Charcoal Gray (#4A4A4A)
Accent: Teal (#2E8B9E)
Background: White (#FFFFFF)
Text: Dark Gray (#333333)
